Welcome

Journalism, “the collection and editing of news for presentation through the media.” For as long as I could remember, my mom would always remind me how she can see me being on T.V one day. The love of technology and media was introduced to me at such a young age. As I became older, my Grandma always says to me she thinks of me while watching stories become executed through reporters and anchors on her favorite local news station. After thinking about all these memories, that is when I knew, storytelling was my passion.
